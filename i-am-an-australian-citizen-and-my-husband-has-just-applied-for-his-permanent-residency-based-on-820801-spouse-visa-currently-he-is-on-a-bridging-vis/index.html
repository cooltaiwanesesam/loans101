Question asked by Sumona (postcode: 4108)

I am an Australian Citizen and my husband has just applied for his permanent residency based on 820/801 spouse visa. Currently he is on a Bridging Visa A that will take him towards the 820 visa [in a couple of months] and then the 801. We would like to apply for a home loan with 5% deposit if we can as we would like to start building a new home shortly. It will also be our first home and we do qualify for FHOG. Would it be possible to secure a home loan?
Gary Lewis - GOFOR Finance (Everton Hills, 4053)
Answered 2017-07-25 00:00:00 +0000 UTC

As Ditte has suggested you can get a loan but we will need more detail to confirm the amount. I have done a very similar one recently and the question is not just whether the Bank will accept your husband as a borrower but also if they will accept his income. At 95% loan there are not too many that do but some will. I am more than happy to go over this with you and explain your options. Free of charge and no obligation.
