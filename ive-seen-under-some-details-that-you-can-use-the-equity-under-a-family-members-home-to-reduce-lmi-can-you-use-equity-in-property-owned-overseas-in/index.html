Question asked by Lisa (postcode: 2017)

I've seen under some details that you can use the equity under a family members home to reduce LMI - can you use equity in property owned overseas in the UK
Trent Stokes - Stokes & Maker Financial Services (Brighton, 3195)
Answered 2017-08-22 00:00:00 +0000 UTC

Equity in an international asset is not acceptable security for Australian lenders. However, another effective means of freeing up funds to reduce or eliminate LMI is a ground approach to your overall financial situation. By taking a wholistic approach to your financial requirements and delving deeper into your circumstances, we can ensure that all facets of your financial situation are performing optimally. If you are servicing debts such as cars, credit cards, personal loans that affect your ability to save effectively and efficiently, a simple financial health check could provide you with the means to better service your loan, save a greater deposit and place you in better overall financial position prior to applying for a home loan.
