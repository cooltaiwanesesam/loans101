Question asked by Amanda (postcode: 2454)

Just wanting to know our options about the possibility of getting a home loan.
Kelvin Mason - Choice Home Loans Castle Hill (Baulkham Hills, 2153)
Answered 2017-07-23 00:00:00 +0000 UTC

What you would need to provide would be your Income - Expenses - Deposit (Equity or Family Guarantee). Income (all people who are borrowing) and details of what it is - salary - commission - family benefits etc. Expenses includes credit card limits, personal loans, car loans, HECS debt. Deposit - savings or any current property - any family member who can guarantee security so you dont need to save for a deposit. What you might also consider - and would pay off any loan quicker - is investing rather than buying to live in. Tax benefits include the rent to help pay the repayments + interest repayments and any costs off your income .
